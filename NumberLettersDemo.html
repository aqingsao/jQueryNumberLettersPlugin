jQueryNumberLettersPlugin

Note: Only numbers(0-9) and letters(a-z, A-Z) are allowed.

Number and Letters

Code: $("input.letters").letters();
Code: $("input.lettersAndSpace").lettersAndSpace();
Code: $("input.numberLetters").numberLetters();
Code: $("input.numberLettersAndSpace").numberLettersAndSpace();
Code: $("input.numbers").numbers();

Common Used Cases

Code: $("input.integer").integer();
Code: $("input.decimal").decimal();
Code: $("input.email").email();
Code: $("input.ip").ip();
Code: $("input.money").money();

How to customize?

Code: $("input.customRegexp").custom("^[a-zA-Z0-9\.-_]*$");;
Code: $("input.lettersWithPaste").letters({pasteAllowed:true});
Code: $("input.validationOnDelete").ip({validationOnDelete: false});
